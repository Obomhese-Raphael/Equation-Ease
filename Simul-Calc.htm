Simultaneous Equation Calculator

Simultaneous equations involve solving multiple equations with multiple unknowns at the same time. They're crucial for understanding relationships between variables and find applications in diverse fields like physics, engineering, and economics. Mastering simultaneous equations enhances problem-solving skills and helps analyze complex systems effectively.
